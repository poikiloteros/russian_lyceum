An intellectual and creative space for Russian-speaking children (2.5 - 14 yrs). We help them become fully bilingual through exploring the world, communication, and art.

The
Format

Sundays. Groups of 6-12.
4-5 subjects combining academic rigor with creative freedom.

01

Russian

Speaking, reading, writing, and literature tailored for active bilinguals.

02

Math

Logic puzzles, arithmetic, and problem-solving via Russian methodology.

03

Science

Experiments, natural world, biology, and foundational physics.

04

Arts

Painting, theatrical performances, music, and emotional intellect.

Moments

Why we
succeed

We create an immersive environment where subjects overlap. The vocabulary learned in Science is reinforced in Art and Russian, mirroring natural language acquisition rather than isolated memorization.

Small group sizes (6-12) ensure personalized attention. We foster deep connections between peers and teachers, building the emotional confidence necessary for uninhibited speaking.

A warm, welcoming atmosphere where making mistakes is celebrated as part of the process. We ensure children associate the Russian language with joy, play, and creativity.
